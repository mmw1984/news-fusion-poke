Major cloud platforms introduced revised storage tiers intended for infrequently accessed enterprise data.

Finance and infrastructure teams are now comparing total cost models, especially for workflows where retrieval latency can be planned in advance.

The biggest impact appears in compliance-heavy sectors with strict retention windows.
