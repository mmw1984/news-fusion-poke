Regulators across multiple EU member states announced a joint implementation timeline for AI governance.

The update focuses on practical compliance guidance for organizations deploying large language models in public-facing services.

Key discussion points include:
- clearer transparency requirements,
- shared audit terminology,
- and a phased reporting process for higher-risk systems.
